Tank bund

Tank Bund in Hyderabad is a popular promenade along Hussain Sagar Lake, connecting Hyderabad and Secunderabad. It is lined with statues of famous personalities and offers a scenic view of the lake, making it a major tourist attraction.

Dhurgam Cheruvu

Durgam Cheruvu, also known as the Secret Lake, is a beautiful freshwater lake located in Hyderabad near Hitech City. Surrounded by granite rocks, it is a popular spot for boating, leisure walks, and enjoying the scenic night view with the cable bridge lighting up the skyline.
